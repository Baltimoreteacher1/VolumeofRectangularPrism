1. Learn the Big Idea

Volume tells how many cubic units fill a 3D shape. A rectangular prism has three dimensions: length, width, and height.

Formula Anchor

How to Solve

  1. 1Find the dimensions. Look for length, width, and height.
  2. 2Write the formula. Use V = l × w × h.
  3. 3Multiply carefully. The order does not matter, but every dimension must be used once.
  4. 4Label cubic units. Volume is measured in units³, such as cm³ or in³.
